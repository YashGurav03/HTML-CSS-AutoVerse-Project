Upcoming Cars of India 2024

Several exciting car launches are set for India in 2024 across various segments:

Maruti Swift 2024

A revamped version of this popular hatchback with an updated design and potential hybrid options is expected mid-year.

Mahindra Thar 5-Door

This highly anticipated larger version of the Thar will retain both petrol and diesel engine options with enhanced space and comfort features. It's expected to launch around March 2024​

Tata Harrier EV

Slated for a late 2024 release, this electric SUV will feature a range exceeding 500 km and an all-wheel-drive option

Kia Carnival 2024

A luxury MPV expected in April 2024, offering a spacious interior, advanced tech like a 12.3-inch infotainment system, and a powerful 2.2L diesel engine​

Hyundai Alcazar Facelift

Expected by the end of 2024, this SUV will feature a refreshed design, updated tech, and both petrol and diesel options
